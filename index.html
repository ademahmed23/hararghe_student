The Hararghe Students Association is a dedicated community of students and alumni from the Hararghe region, united by our commitment to support one another, celebrate our heritage, and create positive change. Founded by students for students, we aim to provide a platform where members can access resources, develop their skills, and find a sense of belonging..

Our mission is to empower students from Hararghe by offering support, opportunities for growth, and a network of peers who share similar goals and backgrounds. We are dedicated to fostering a sense of pride in our heritage and encouraging members to pursue their academic and professional dreams.

Vision

We envision a connected community of Hararghe students who are equipped to succeed and ready to make meaningful contributions both locally and globally. By building strong foundations in education, culture, and collaboration, we strive to create lasting impact and inspire future generations..

What We Do

The Hararghe Students Association organizes events, workshops, and cultural celebrations that reflect our values and promote learning, personal growth, and unity. Key initiatives include:

  • Educational Support: Providing academic resources, study groups, and mentorship for members.
  • Cultural Events: Hosting events that celebrate Hararghe heritage and bring our community closer together.
  • Networking Opportunities: Creating connections between current students, alumni, and professionals.

Whether you’re a current student, alumni, or supporter, we welcome you to be part of our journey. Together, we’re building a brighter future for Hararghe students and strengthening our shared roots. Join us to learn, grow, and make a difference!
